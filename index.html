About AeroBMSCE

Our Mission

To foster innovation and excellence in the fields of aeromodelling, drone technology, and RC aviation. We aim to provide students a platform to design, build, and fly real-world aerial systems while developing their technical and creative skills through collaborative teamwork and competition.

Our Vision

To be a leading aeromodelling community that inspires future engineers to revolutionize the skies through sustainable, efficient, and creative aerial technologies. We envision creating a culture of hands-on learning, exploration, and interdisciplinary innovation at BMSCE and beyond.
